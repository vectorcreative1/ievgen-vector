Żubrówka

Go Wild

New communication platform for Żubrówka brand

The Problem

Żubrówka is one of the world's most iconic vodkas — the third largest globally, after Absolut and Smirnoff — with its signature serve, Apple Żu, enjoyed across many countries. However, to keep growing, the brand needed to reconnect with a younger generation that was increasingly choosing other spirits.

The Solution

We took Żubrówka out of the Białowieża Forest and into the untamed energy of the modern city. We transformed the brand's voice and redesigned its visual identity around emotion rather than heritage. Inspired by the wildness that has always been part of Żubrówka's DNA, we reframed it as the untamed nature that lives within each of us — waiting to be unleashed.
