Oby Było Chore

CSR campaign

The Problem

Future parents and their circle often repeat, "Oby było zdrowe" ("may it be healthy"). Unfortunately, in reality, many mothers behave the opposite. In Poland, 9,000 children are born with FAS (Fetal Alcohol Syndrome) each year.

The Solution

In our campaign we show the absurdity of such behavior in a strong and shocking manner.
