Vectra

New Communication Platform

New communication platform and using AI for optimising production process

The Problem

Following the merger with Multimedia Polska, Vectra became the largest cable TV operator in Poland. Despite this new market position, the brand identity was outdated, inconsistent, and no longer reflected its scale or ambition. The visual system lacked coherence and a clear, modern expression.

The Solution

We redefined Vectra's identity with a modern, energetic branding system. We replaced celebrity-based communication with a proprietary set of 3D characters, building a distinctive and ownable brand world. We also established a consistent color system and unified visual language across all touchpoints.
